Mission Statement

BAHANDi promotes global biodiversity and sustainable land use by empowering local communities to manage and conserve natural resources within their social, cultural, and economic context. Our goal is to encourage the widespread adoption of community-based conservation efforts.

Vision

BAHANDi imagines a future where natural areas are safeguarded to support diverse wildlife and provide enduring enjoyment for people. Our focus on education, collaboration, and ethical stewardship aims to preserve biodiversity, create local job opportunities, and raise awareness among tourists about the value of conservation
